A/B Testing

A/B testing is widely used in intustry. It is part of what we commonly call "experiment Design".

Putting it simply, The objective of an experiment design is to figure out if the change of a feature would results in a change of another feature. To illustrate, A corporation may decide to test different pages designs. In medecine, research may want to test or experiment patent reactions to ovel drugs

I - Experiment Design

To understand the process of experiment design, it is important to know some of the fundemental concept and the statistical techniques used in the process

1- Experiment concepts
Images that a fictional company name amazonic engineering team are discusing on a potential shopping pages changes. The discussion revolves around a specific feature that will display a different color blue as compared to an original yellow color. A experiment would help answer the following question. Does the change in color drives more sale? To helps illustrate how experiment design works, let's define the types of experiment that we are commonly used.

Based on the amount of control of the feature that are available, two keys aspect determine whether there is an experiment or not. Note that there are others that will not be explain in this article. Assuming we splited the customers into two main groups, we must first have the possibilty to estabilish a comparison beetween group A and group B. Second, we must be have control over other variables.. If the two concepts are not attainable, then we can not implement an experiment.

As noted above, if we have a higher control over the features, we can have an experiment. However, there are instances in which, there is no group or we are unable to complete a random sampling. In these types of scenarios, we can use alternate methods such as observational study. Observational study is used when we mainly don't have control over the features that we have.
eg. A medical team collected patients with type 1 dibetese data to compare the effect that the type of exercise completed after nutrition and guclose level (Observational study). Amazonic decided to send a coupons codes to some "primy" members (Experiment).
1-1 Other Types of Experiment
There are two types of experiment that we can implement, one that allow each group to only see oe version of the pages. If group A is asigned to interact with the yellow webpage mentioned above, they will not be able to use the webpage with blue color. The goes for group B. This is often called Beetween-subjects design. The other method is called Within-subjects design and it allow both group to interact with the two designs. In this process, we can show group A the yellow page first then blue next and we reverse the other for group B. It is important to note that the custmers are randomnly selected.
